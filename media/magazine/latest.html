HARD LESSONS

I Lost €3,200 in Crypto — Here's Exactly What I Did Wrong

Transparency is rare in this space. Everyone shows wins. Here are the exact mistakes that cost me €3,200 — so you can avoid them.

**The trades that hurt**

Mistake #1: I FOMO'd into a meme coin at the top.
A coin called PEPE went 2,000% in 48 hours. I saw it on Twitter. I bought at the peak. It dropped 80% in 6 hours. Loss: €400. Lesson: never buy after a 1,000%+ move. That's exit liquidity.

Mistake #2: I didn't set a stop-loss.
I was holding a mid-cap altcoin with a great project. No stop-loss set. Market crashed 35% overnight during a macro event (Fed rate decision). I froze. Watched it drop from €1,800 to €800 before I reacted. Loss: €1,000. Lesson: always set a stop at -15% when you enter. No exceptions.

Mistake #3: I traded with borrowed money.
I used a small amount of leverage — 3x. 'Only 3x,' I thought. A 33% move wiped me out completely. Loss: €800. Lesson: never trade crypto with leverage unless you've done it for 2+ years with small amounts first.

Mistake #4: I trusted a 'signal group.'
Paid €50/month for 'expert signals.' Followed them blindly. They were pump-and-dump promoters. Loss: €1,000 in bad trades before I figured it out. Lesson: no one can predict markets. Anyone selling signals is selling false confidence.

**What I do now:**
- 2% max portfolio risk per trade
- Always set stop-loss before entering
- No leverage
- No signal groups
- Research every coin myself before buying

I recovered all of this and more. But only after I stopped making these mistakes.
