Meditation Techniques Guide
🫁
Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)
⏱ 5 minutes
Inhale 4s → Hold 4s → Exhale 4s → Hold 4s. Activates parasympathetic nervous system within 60 seconds. Used by Navy SEALs to achieve calmness under extreme stress.
Science: HRV +18%, cortisol -12% in 5 min sessions
👁️
Open Awareness Meditation
⏱ 10–20 minutes
Observe all sensations, thoughts, and sounds without attachment. No object focus. The most studied advanced technique for reducing mind-wandering and increasing present-moment awareness.
Science: DMN deactivation 34% greater than breath focus alone
🌊
Yoga Nidra / NSDR
⏱ 20 minutes
Conscious sleep. Lie down, follow a body scan, transition to hypnagogic state while maintaining awareness. 20-minute session = 4h sleep equivalent for dopamine restoration.
Science: Dopamine levels restored equivalent to full night's sleep
💗
Loving-Kindness (Metta)
⏱ 10 minutes
Systematically direct compassion toward self, loved ones, neutral people, difficult people, and all beings. Most effective technique for reducing social anxiety and improving relationships.
Science: Vagal tone +29%, social connection scores +41%
🔥
Wim Hof Breathing
⏱ 15 minutes
30 deep breath cycles → hold → recovery breath. Alkalizes blood pH, floods cells with oxygen, triggers controlled stress hormesis. Do only lying down — no driving.
Science: Immune response to endotoxin suppressed by 50%
🌅
Morning Sunlight Gaze
⏱ 10 minutes
Look toward sunrise (never directly at sun) within 30 min of waking. Entrains cortisol peak, sets serotonin/melatonin timing, and is the single most powerful circadian regulator available.
Science: Serotonin +42%, sleep quality +37% in 2 weeks
