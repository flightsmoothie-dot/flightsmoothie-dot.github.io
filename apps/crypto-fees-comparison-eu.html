Hidden Fees to Watch For

  • Spread on market orders — "Simple" interfaces on many exchanges add a spread (the difference between buy and sell price) on top of stated fees. Always check both.
  • Network (gas) fees — When you withdraw crypto to your own wallet, you pay a blockchain network fee. These vary by congestion and are not controlled by the exchange.
  • Inactivity fees — Some exchanges charge monthly fees if your account is dormant. Always check the fee schedule.
  • Conversion fees — If you deposit EUR and the exchange converts it to USD before trading, you pay a conversion spread both ways.
  • Leverage/margin fees — Borrowing to trade is very expensive on all platforms. Avoid leverage as a beginner.
