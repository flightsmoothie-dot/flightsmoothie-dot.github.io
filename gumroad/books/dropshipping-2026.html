Chapter 1

Why Dropshipping Still Works (And Why Most People Fail)

Everyone declares dropshipping dead every year. Every year, new people quietly build €10,000/month stores doing it. The technology changes. The principles don't.

Dropshipping is simple: you sell products online that a supplier ships directly to your customer. You never touch inventory. You make the margin between what you sell for and what the supplier charges.

€218B
Global dropshipping market size in 2026 (Statista) — still growing at 24% annually

Why 95% Fail

  • Wrong product: Selling what they like instead of what has proven demand
  • Wrong pricing: Not accounting for ad spend, returns, and fees in margins
  • Wrong traffic: Expecting free traffic before earning it
  • Wrong timeline: Expecting profit in week 1 instead of month 3
  • Wrong mindset: Treating it as a lottery ticket instead of a real business
✅
The Winning Formula: High-margin product + proven demand + reliable supplier + adequate ad budget + patience for 90 days. Simple. Not easy.
Chapter 2

Product Research: Finding Winners Before Competitors

The product is 80% of the business. A mediocre store with a great product beats a great store with a mediocre product every time.

What Makes a Winning Dropship Product

  • Solves a real, specific problem
  • Not available in every local store (reduces price comparison)
  • Retail price €25–€150 (enough margin, not too much friction to buy)
  • Lightweight and small (shipping costs stay manageable)
  • Not easily breakable (returns kill margins)
  • Has a passionate, identifiable audience

The Research Process

Step 1: TikTok product research. Search "#TikTokMadeMeBuyIt" and filter by recent. What products are going viral? This is the fastest free signal for trending demand.

Step 2: AliExpress trending. Go to AliExpress → "Bestselling." Filter by orders in the last 30 days. Look for products with 1,000+ orders but still manageable review counts (meaning they're growing, not saturated).

Step 3: Competitor research. Find stores already selling in your niche. Use SimilarWeb to check their traffic. If they're getting 50,000+ visits/month, the niche has proven demand.

The €0 Validation Trick
Create a simple product page and run €20 of Facebook traffic to it. If you get clicks (not even purchases), the concept has pull. If nobody clicks, move on before spending more.

Niches With Strong 2026 Margins

🏋️ Fitness & Recovery
Massage guns, posture correctors, home gym accessories. High perceived value, passionate buyers.
🐾 Pet Products
Pet owners spend emotionally. High AOV, strong repeat purchase, endless sub-niches.
🏠 Home Organization
Storage, kitchen gadgets, cleaning tools. YouTube/TikTok organic potential is huge.
🧘 Wellness & Self-Care
Essential oils, sleep aids, stress relief. High markup potential, emotional buyers.
Chapter 3

The Store Setup That Actually Converts

Most dropshipping stores are built to look like stores. Winning stores are built to convert. These are not the same thing.

Platform Choice: Shopify vs. WooCommerce

For beginners: Shopify. €29/month. Handles everything. Don't waste time building on WooCommerce until you're generating €5,000/month — then the savings justify the complexity.

The Minimum Viable Store

  • Clean theme (Dawn is free and converts well)
  • High-quality product images (order the product and photograph it yourself)
  • Benefit-focused copy (not features — benefits)
  • Clear shipping timeline promise (be honest)
  • Money-back guarantee (reduces purchase anxiety dramatically)
  • At least 5 genuine reviews before launching
💡
Conversion Rate Reality Check: A good dropshipping store converts at 2-3%. An average one is at 0.5-1%. If your conversion rate is below 1%, fix the store before running more ads.

Trust Signals That Matter Most

1. Real photos — not AliExpress stock images that buyers can find with reverse image search
2. Response time — "Replies within 2 hours" visible on the store
3. Specific guarantee — "60-day money back, no questions" is stronger than "100% satisfaction"
4. Founder story — a real About page with a real person builds trust exponentially

Chapter 4

Traffic & Ads: The Fuel That Runs It

No traffic, no sales. This is the part most courses underteach because it's the hardest part.

The Traffic Progression for Dropshipping

Phase 1 (Month 1): TikTok organic. Post 3 videos/day showing the product in use. No investment needed. If a video hits 100k views, that's your first validation.

Phase 2 (Month 2): Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram). Start with €15/day broad targeting. Let the algorithm find your buyers. Don't touch campaigns for 72 hours after launch — data needs to accumulate.

Phase 3 (Month 3+): Scale what works. If a Meta ad is profitable at €15/day, scale to €50, then €150. Cut losers ruthlessly. Feed winners.

⚠️
The Math Must Work First: If your product costs €15, you sell for €45, and Meta ads cost €25 to acquire a customer, your gross profit is €5. That's not sustainable. Fix margins before scaling ad spend.

The Metric That Matters Most

ROAS (Return on Ad Spend). Target 3x minimum on Meta. That means for every €1 in ads, you generate €3 in revenue. At your product margins, this typically means profitability. Below 2.5x ROAS: pause and fix the funnel.
